PearlHacks Director of Technology

    Starting in May of 2022, I worked as the Director of Technology for PearlHacks. PearlHacks is a hackathon hosted at UNC-CH every year specifically for women and gender non-conforming individuals. As Director of Technology I was responsible for creating and maintaining the PearlHacks website.

  • My primary responsibility was to create and maintain the PearlHacks website. This meant I needed to take the old website and redesign it to make it fit the theme for next year's hackathon. Working closely with the Director of Design, we revamped the website to include a new color pallette, logos, mascot, theme, and more.

    In addition to the design of the website, I made sure that participants and volunteers would be able to register for the event. Participants and volunteers would also be able to find important information and resources on the website such athe schedule and guides on how to get started for first time hackers.

    Sponsors for the event also needed somewhere to find the information about different forms and due dates they had for the event. I created a password protected website that allowed sponsors to access that information as well as a resume database.

    Lastly, I helped run the event on the day of which included last minute website updates, annoucements, and logistical matters such as providing hackers with outlets and distributing meals.
