Mechanical Keyboards

One of my hobbies is building my own custom mechanical keyboards. For a custom mechanical keyboard you can choose everything from the key layout, form factor, switch type, keycap shape, and more. All these factors lead to different sounding and feeling keyboards and it's just so much fun to me. From this hobby I've not only got a hole in my wallet but also learned how to solder and an understanding of how the minor details can lead to massive changes.
