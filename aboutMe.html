About Me

Background

  • My name is Robert Gan (he/him/his) and I'm an explorer, creator, and developer that loves figuring out how things work and taking weird ideas and making them a reality. From the beginning, I've always loved taking apart anything I could get my hands on to figure out how they work from a simplet button to mice and keyboards. Although some of those objects never got put back together, I discovered a passion for engineering that would lead me to where I am today.

Education

    I currently attend the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill as a junior pursuing a BS in Computer Science and a minor in Applied Sciences and Engineering.

Hobbies and Interests

You can find me playing sports like volleyball, ultimate frisbee, tennis, rock wall climbing, and more. If I'm not playing sports I'm probably at the makerspace working on the next iteration of a project or at home playing some video games to relax.
