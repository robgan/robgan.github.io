ClipNZip

    In my spring 2022 APPL 412 class, Making Idea Into Reality, we were challenged to find a real world problem and design and create a solution for that problem. The problem that my team and I ended up finding was that for individuals with limited dexterity zippers are challenging. This problem was rather personal to our team because of family members who experience this issue on a daily basis so we had first-hand testmimonies on the problems with zippers. Although they seem like such an easy thing for adults zippers are require coordination in both hands to link the two halves together as well as a certain amount of force to pull the zipper upwards. Even a small amount of dexterity loss can lead individuals not being able to use a zipper. Dexterity loss in quite common in the population between old age as well as medical conditions such as Parkinsons, cerebral palsy, and more.

  • We started off with conducting interviews with individuals with dexterity issues to understand what the problem was and exactly where they struggled when it came to zippers. It turned out the answer was rather complex. For children it was more of a cognitive issue in understanding the correct steps to get a zipper pulled up. For those with dexterity issues it was that they couldn't use both arms or couldn't exert the force needed to hold down the bottom of their jacket to get the zipper started.

    From the information we gathered we started brainstorming different ideas and created rough prototypes until we discovered something that seemed like it would work. The idea was a device that would clip to the bottom edge of a jacket. A user would then step on a portion of the device and a retractable cord would pull the device tight and would keep one side of the jacket taut while they used one arm to zip up their jacket.

  • At the end of the year we presented our project to the UNC BeAM Makerfest where my team and I won 3rd place in the Most Creative Category.

ClipNZip

Features include:

  • Retractable cord
  • Quick release clip to attatch to jacket
  • Flexible foot strap
  • Durable design
  • Easily replacable parts
  • Pocketable size
  • Works univerally on jackets
